Hey there, fellow wallpaper lovers! Over the years, I’ve fallen head over heels for wallpaper and collected countless samples for my home and other projects. So today, I want to share my absolute favorite floral wallpaper designs with you, and also clear up some common questions about both peel and stick and traditional wallpaper. Let’s get started!

I’m mixing both peel and stick and traditional wallpaper in my list of favorites, so keep that in mind when considering what might work best for your space.

Is Peel and Stick Wallpaper More Expensive?

Traditional wallpaper tends to be less expensive overall, but many people find peel and stick options easier to install themselves, which can save on installation costs. From my experience, wallpaper installation can range from $500 to $2,000 depending on the project size.

FAQ

What is the most affordable place to buy wallpaper?

One way to save is to shop where you can use coupons, like Anthropologie (hello, 20% off codes!). Etsy and Target also have great budget-friendly options.

Should I install wallpaper myself?

Personally, I prefer saving up for a professional install because I’m not a perfectionist in that way. But both peel and stick and traditional wallpaper can be DIY-friendly if you watch some YouTube tutorials and have patience.

What kind of wallpaper alternatives do you recommend?

If you’re on a tight budget, consider one of our statement wall DIYs (some cost under $10 for supplies!). You can mimic nearly any wallpaper look if you’re up for a DIY project.

I’m a renter—how do I know if the wallpaper will come off without damaging my walls?

I’ve had walls damaged by both peel and stick and traditional wallpaper, and also had experiences with both types that came off perfectly. I recommend buying a sample, sticking it on your wall, then removing it to check for damage.

Can you put wallpaper in a bathroom?

Yes, I’ve done it before! It’s suitable for a powder room or low-traffic bathroom. For high-moisture bathrooms with daily showers, I’d avoid wallpaper unless it’s specifically moisture-resistant.
